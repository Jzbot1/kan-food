# Enterprise Multi-Vendor Food Delivery SaaS Platform

Build a production-ready, enterprise-grade Multi-Vendor Food Delivery SaaS Platform inspired by Zomato, Swiggy, Uber Eats, DoorDash, Deliveroo, Foodpanda, Talabat, GrabFood, and Glovo.

The platform must support multiple restaurants, restaurant branches, customers, delivery partners, and a Super Admin from a single codebase.

Every restaurant should have its own dashboard, subscription, staff, menu, orders, analytics, wallet, and settings.

The platform owner (Super Admin) controls everything.

This is NOT a demo project.

Everything must be production-ready.
